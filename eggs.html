Here is how you can make perfect eggs!

Description

Here is a recipe for some perfect, fluffy scrambled eggs perfect
have as a main protien source alongside any complete and balanced
breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Non-stick pan
  • Non-stick spray/oil (butter can work just as well
  • A plastic spatula
  • Three thicc eggs
  • Some milk
  • salt, pepper, along with your favorite seasonings
  • Any garnish

Steps

  1. look yourself in the mirror and ask yourself
    are you ready?
  2. Heat your pan on medium heat and while it is heating, Begin to prep your ingredients
    • Crack your eggs into a bowl
    • Whisk until it feels like a massive bowl of yellow nut
    • Resist the urge to just chug the whole bowl
    • Slowly add milk while whisking a medium pace until
      the color of the eggs has lightenened to a rich yellow
    • Here you can add diced bell peppers, tomatoes, etc
  3. spray some non-stick on the pan, ideally, it shouldn't burn
    when it touches the pan, but don't worry if it does
  4. Add egg mixture and begin wait til you see a lighter color form around the brim of the pan
  5. Once a consistent ring of lighter yellow has formed around the pan
    use the spatula to drag the edges of the pan towards the middle
  6. Continue to do this and pay attention to how the eggs cook
  7. Every 30 seconds, raise the pan off of the heat for 5 seconds as you
    continue to drag the cooked edges towards the middle
  8. While you are doing this, ask yourself why doesn't your father love you
  9. As soon as the eggs are fully solidified and there isn't any pure liquid left
    in the pan, remove the pan from heat and transfer to a plate. This ensures you
    don't overcook the eggs. You want them to retain a soft texture with some shine to it.
    You never want to overcook the eggs until they are a rubbery texture. Remove from heat
    when you see there is no liquid left, but the eggs are just barely like a clumpy, gel texture.
  10. Add garnish and consume with the rest of your beautiful breakfast.
